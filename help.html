The aim of Inspirotype is to improve your typing speed and technique while expanding your mind with some inspiring and thought-provoking quotes.

Instructions:

  1. Type 10 quotes as fast as you can
  2. Get your average W.P.M.(words per minute)
  3. Try and beat your score
Easy Mode

* No capital letters or punctuation

Hard Mode

* Capital letters and all punctuation required

* Two spaces after full stop, One space after all other punctuation

Typing Tips:

  • Focus on accuracy. Speed will come with practice
  • Take the time to learn the ASDF typing technique
